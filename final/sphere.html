Spherical Journey


This was a collaborative project by myself and two other classmates. The assignment was to successfully take a six foot sphere through MassArt and back, stoping at a few designated checkpoints.
Our group chose to make a handmade map because it gives the user a special feeling that someone crafted this book just for them, and we thought it facilitated the understanding of steps, since some are a bit tricky. Our group also quickly learned the meaning of a revision- we have made about 6 "draft" books.
Information Architecture I. Massart 2013.
