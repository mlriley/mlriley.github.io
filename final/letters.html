Gonzo


Hand lettering I created for a paper I wrote about the Hunter S. Thompson documentary, "Gonzo". It embodies some of the drawing qualities of Ralph Steadman's work, who made the majority of images for Hunter's novels. Ink.












Handcrafted Letterforms


This was one of my first typography assignments. We were given the names of typefaces and were required to find the elements of a letter that make it unique to that certain typeface. Black and white gouache on bristol.
