FURR MAGAZINE


Furr Magazine is a one-of-a-kind, fictional magazine about animal science. If this magazine were to be mass produced, it would be published bi-yearly with each issue showcasing a different fur covered animal. This Winter 2014 issue is focusing solely on the dog and how
it's brain functions similarly to our own. The goal of Furr is to combine your average animal magazine with a design magazine in order to get the user more excited about the content. (But come on, who doesn't love dogs, right?)
