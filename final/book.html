Underground Virtues


Underground Virtues is a book made up of three design essays by Gui Bonsieppe, Jan Van Toorn, and Steven Heller. The large graphic numbers and overall boldness is speaking to what the writers are talking about regarding design, and different pull quotes are weaved into similar sections.
