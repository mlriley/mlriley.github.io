BOOKMARKING


For this project, I took a database of 100 things and make it visual, yet also organize it by another seven catagories. The categories I organized my books by were the embarrasing stage of my life that I was in (which was pretty easy to figure out based on the amount of vampire books I have), title, author, length, genre, satisfactory level, favorites, and if I read it for school or leisure.
