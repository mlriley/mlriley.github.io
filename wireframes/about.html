Graphic Designer | Boston

Her work consists of mostly hand crafted vector art, photography, or a mix, along with a large amount of bold typography to accompany it. She has interned for Bow & Drape, a start up company specializing in customizable online women’s clothing. Alongside that, she has worked at various cafes and now can write “Happy Birthday” on any given pastry and make nearly one million and a half coffee drinks.
